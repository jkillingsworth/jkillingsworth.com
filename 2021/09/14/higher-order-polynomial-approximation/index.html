High­er Or­der Poly­no­mi­al Ap­prox­i­ma­tion

This is an ex­ten­sion of one of my ear­li­er posts on poly­no­mi­al ap­prox­i­ma­tions. Pre­vi­ous­ly, I demon­strat­ed how to find ap­prox­i­mate so­lu­tions to the weight­ed coin toss prob­lem us­ing first, sec­ond, and third or­der poly­no­mi­als to de­scribe the weights of the bi­ased coin­s. In this post, I demon­strate a gen­er­al­ized method for ap­ply­ing this tech­nique us­ing high­er or­der poly­no­mi­al­s.
